Our Roadmap

Discover the key milestones of our project and our vision for the future of the automotive industry.

Phase 1: Initial Launch (0-6 months)

- Development and launch of the official Carify website.
- Deployment of the $CARI token on the BSC blockchain.
- Strategic presale lasting approximately 2 months to fund the project.
- Community engagement through targeted communication campaigns across social media and official channels.

Phase 2: Development and Partnerships (6-18 months)

- Launch of the technology linking each vehicle to a unique NFT with a verifiable history.
- Certification of NFTs to include mileage, repairs, and ownership details.
- Establishing partnerships with automotive manufacturers, garages, and blockchain experts.
- Strengthening the team with additional blockchain developers and project managers.

Phase 3: Marketplace Launch (18-24 months)

- Launch of a dedicated marketplace for buying, selling, and exchanging NFT license plates.
- Introduction of features like history verification and component certification.
- Collaboration with artists to design unique and personalized NFT license plates.

Phase 4: Adoption and Diversification (24-36 months)

- Development of physical license plates linked to NFTs for seamless traceability.
- Pilot testing with governments and regulatory bodies to assess integration into official systems.
- Launch of an insurance service tailored to vehicles equipped with NFT license plates, ensuring their protection and compliance.

Phase 5: Global Adoption and Innovation (Beyond 36 months)

- Expansion into international markets with a focus on adapting to local regulations.
- Partnerships with governments for the official adoption of NFT license plates.
